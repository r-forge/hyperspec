Extended binary arithmetic operators

hyperSpec’s binary arithmetic operators (+, -, *, /) now working for calculations on spectra matrix plus row or column-vector objects as well, so that many calls to sweep can now be more easily expressed:

> plot (flu - colMeans (flu))
> plot (flu / rowMeans (flu))
